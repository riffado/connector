You're set up.

The Riffado Connector is installed. Here's how to use it — it takes about thirty seconds.

  1. 1

    Open Riffado

    Go to your Riffado instance — the hosted version at riffado.com, or your self-hosted URL.

  2. 2

    Click "Continue with Plaud"

    On the Riffado connect screen you'll see a Continue with Plaud button. The extension adds it automatically — you don't need to enable anything.

  3. 3

    Sign in to Plaud as you normally would

    A new tab opens at web.plaud.ai. Sign in with Google, Apple, or email — whatever you already use. The extension never sees your password. Once Plaud has you logged in, the tab closes automatically and Riffado takes over.
